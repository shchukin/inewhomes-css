About

Due to the neighborhood’s immense potential for growth, Sunny Isles have become a haven for newly constructed properties.Sunny Isles new construction are mostly old, single family homes that were converted to become high rise condominium complexes, which are aimed at highlighting the natural beauty of the neighborhood.

Most of the Sunny Isles new constructions are waterfront allowing residents to have daily views of the Atlantic Ocean and the Intracoastal Waterways. At night, homeowners can bask at the beautiful city lights of Downtown Miami and the rest of the City of Miami.

Intricate façade, carefully designed interiors and spacious units with great amenities and services truly make Sunny Isles new constructions the jewel of Miami.
